Time management and gaining new habits

Time Management Definition

“Time management” is the process of organizing and planning how to divide your time between specific activities. Good time management enables you to work smarter – not harder – so that you get more done in less time, even when time is tight and pressures are high. Failing to manage your time damages your effectiveness and causes stress. source

So far, I tried pomodoro timebox technique, it works for me and I want to keep using it. I also tried Daily Scrum and spent 15 min planning attack for the daily tasks in the morning. I'm planning to keep using the techniques, work in small iterations and to note time spent on different tasks.

The challenge might be conditioning myself and creating a habit of making breaks off the desk, which is important for my back pain.
