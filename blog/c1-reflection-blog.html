Reflections

I have a positive attitude towards EDA and my learning at the academy

This type of culture makes me feel comfortable. It seems more relaxed and focused at the same time than the IT culture I know from my country. Also, it looks like the school provides real and natural learning environment. This is great opportunity for people without commercial experience.

I am very excited about the training. We will have lots of fun when working together in the kohort. Everyone is at a bit different level and have their challenges but those things change fast. We can already learn from each other and I am sure that everyone will be flying soon.

My expectations from EDA have not changed and to be honest, I do have more expectations from myself than from EDA.
