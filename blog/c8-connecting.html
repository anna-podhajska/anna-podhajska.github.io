Complete the political awareness exercise here in writing.

Situation - my very old company grew, and the number of business travel increased significantly. Budget spent on business travel was already counted in millions, it was incredibly difficult to manage all trips for all company sites, there was a need for travel process and tools improvements.

My team's objective was improving travel-related processes in order to manage the growing travel numbers. Long story short, one of many things needed at the time was replacing an old ticketing system with a new one. The old system that was used and adopted by travel desk was designed for other purpose, could not handle reports, and had lots of other limitations. If any improvements were possible then they were time-taking, full of bugs, and required work-arounds.

The company understood the need of improvement. However it was also dealing with other issues at the time, it was close to selling one of the departments, and additional savings had to be made in all areas. Despite initial approval, it was not possible to buy a new system at that time.
