My objective was improving travel-related processes in order to manage the growing travel numbers and reduce their cost. Long story short, one of many things needed at the time was replacing an old ticketing system with a new one. The old system had lots of limitations, and if any improvements were possible then they were not easy to make and required work-arounds.

The company understood the need of improvement. However it was also dealing with other issues at the time, it was close to selling one of the departments, and additional savings had to be made in all areas. Despite initial approval, it was not possible to buy a new system at the time.
