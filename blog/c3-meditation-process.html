Meditation

Chapter 2, "Search inside yourself" by Chade Meng-Tan, Google.

What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

I thought that it is more difficult to start.

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

It is possible to meditate in different positions. It is also important to acknowledge any stymuli that arises during the meditation, accept it and if we really need to react then postpone the reaction by 5 breaths in order to increase the time period between the impuls and reaction.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

Mindfulness meditatio when lying down, eyes half open seem the best for me.

Any other musings?

I like that it is not that hard to start, and comparing meditation to sweating at the gym, just withoiut sweating, and without gym. Brain is like a mussle, need practice in order to achieve its default state, which is calmness and happiness.
